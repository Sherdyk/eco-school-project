Our School and Beautiful Zalishchyky

Welcome to the Zalishchyky State Gymnasium! We are proud to lead this eco-project from our incredible hometown. Zalishchyky is a unique and picturesque city, famously situated on a peninsula formed by a sweeping meander of the Dniester River. Surrounded by such spectacular nature, we feel a deep responsibility to protect our environment.

This project is designed to promote an eco-friendly lifestyle. Watch our video to see how we protect nature through daily actions.
