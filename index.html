This project is about promoting an eco-friendly lifestyle in school. Watch our video to see how we protect nature through daily actions.

Main Values

Respect for Nature

Understanding that we are part of the ecosystem and must protect it.

Responsibility

Taking ownership of our waste and daily environmental choices.

Teamwork

Working together as students and teachers to reach sustainable goals.

Actions in School & Local Nature

We research local biodiversity, specifically the Dniester Canyon, one of the natural wonders of Ukraine.

Red Book Plants of Our Region

Endangered

Snowdrop (Galanthus)

A delicate flower found in the forests near the canyon. It requires strict protection.

Endangered

Pulsatilla (Pasqueflower)

Rare species blooming on the sunny slopes of the Dniester Canyon.

Eco Challenges

Join our mission! Small actions create a big impact. Here is what you can do:

  • Zero Waste Week: Try using no plastic bags for 7 days.
  • Paper Recycling: Collect and bring old paper to the school collection point.
  • Energy Saver: Make sure all lights and screens are off after lessons.
